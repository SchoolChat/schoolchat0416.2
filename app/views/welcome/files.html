・Theory of Knowledge (TOK/知の理論）
  • 　　宿題：
  • 　　・How is history similar to science and how certain is history?
  • 　　　　事例：Mayan calendar and its discussed prophesies for 2012
  • 　　最終エッセイ：
  • 　　・In what ways may disagreement aid the pursuit of knowledge in the natural and human sciences?
  • 　　・"The possession of knowledge carries ethical responsibility." Evaluate this statement.
  • 　　最終プレゼン：
  • 　　 ・How reliable is statistical data in human sciences given the issue of selection bias?
  • 　　　　原稿+スライド等
  • 　　　　事例：新聞会社による統計調査の手法
  • 　　　　時間：３０分
